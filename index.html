Bespoke solutions for industrial, commercial and domestic sectors.


Services

With over 40 years experience in the industry, Green Heath provides inventive solutions to deliver energy-efficient economical solutions for your heating cooling and ventilation requirements. Our strength is in design and installation of bespoke systems, specifically tailored to match our clients requirements.

Air-conditioning / Comfort Cooling

Design, supply and installation of commercial and domestic air conditioning systems. Including chilled water systems, VRV, VRF, single and multiple split systems and close control systems. Systems can be designed to include pollen filtration and air purification.

Heating

Design, supply and installation of commercial and domestic heating systems. Including air source heat pumps, modular boiler systems, gas fired radiant heating, heat exchangers and combined heat and power (CHP).

Project Management

Management of small or large projects with full Construction Design and Management (CDM).

Ventilation

Design, supply and installation of commercial and domestic ventilation systems for a range of environments; commercial kitchens, hotels, swimming pools, fume cabinets, factories and offices.

Servies include kitchen extraction, fume extraction, dust extraction, humidity control, forced air cooling and passive ventilation systems. Systems can be designed to include pollen filtration, air purification and heat exchangers.

With over 40 years knowledge and experience leading my own companies in the mechanical and electrical industry we are able to provide the complete package from site survey, solution options, design, professional installation and maintenance contracts for both commercial, industrial and domestic clients.

Over the years we have designed and installed be spoke ventilation, heating and air conditioning solutions for private and public sector including Hilton Hotels, Marriot, NHS, schools, local authorities, restaurants, small businesses and domestic homes. See Gallery.

We take pride in finding solutions that not only solve your problem but takes into account the need for energy efficiency, appearance and sound levels.
